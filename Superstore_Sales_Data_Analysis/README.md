# 📊 Superstore Sales Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on performing **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on the *Sample Superstore dataset* using **Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn**.

The goal is to analyze sales, profit, customer segments, and regional performance to extract meaningful business insights through data visualization.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Perform data cleaning and preprocessing
* Analyze sales and profit trends
* Identify high-performing and loss-making areas
* Use data visualization to uncover insights
* Apply groupby operations for business analysis

---

## 🔧 Data Cleaning Steps

* Checked and handled missing values
* Removed duplicate records
* Converted date columns into proper datetime format

## 📌 Key Insights

* High sales do not always result in high profit due to discounts
* Technology category is the most profitable
* Furniture category shows lower profit margins
* High discounts lead to losses
* Consumer segment contributes the most revenue
* West and East regions perform better
* Sales show seasonal trends
* Few states dominate overall sales

---

## 📊 Business Summary

* Profitability is highly impacted by discount strategies
* Focus should be on optimizing pricing and cost structure
* Improve performance in low-performing regions and categories
* Strengthen high-performing segments for business growth
